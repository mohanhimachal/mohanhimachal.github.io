About Me

Professional Journey

As a dedicated educational leader with 29 years of experience teaching Mathematics, I've devoted my career to creating engaging learning environments that drive student progress and achievement.

My journey in education has been marked by numerous milestones, including being recognized as a Fulbright Fellow in 2020, receiving the State Teacher Award in 2024, and being acknowledged as a Valuable Contributor in Mathematics at the National Level by NCERT.

I'm passionate about advancing digital literacy among rural students and have guided over 6000 educators across Himachal Pradesh as a resource person. My focus on ICT integration in classrooms, innovative teaching strategies, and teacher training has helped transform educational practices in my region.
